# DAM Mentor CRM — Usage Guide

Welcome to the DAM Mentor CRM. This guide covers everything you need to find mentors, track outreach, and manage your connections.

**CRM URL:** https://crm.damfellows.com

Ask Krish for your login credentials if you don't have them yet.

---

## Getting Started

### Logging In
1. Go to https://crm.damfellows.com
2. Sign in with your email and password (or Google if enabled)

### Connecting Your Gmail
To send emails and track conversations from within the CRM:
1. Go to **Settings** (gear icon, bottom left) > **Accounts**
2. Click **Connect with Google**
3. Sign in with your DAM Google account and grant permissions

Once connected, your email conversations with contacts will automatically appear on their records.

---

## Finding Mentors

### People View
Click **People** in the left sidebar to see all 1300+ contacts. By default you'll see a table view.

### Filtering
Use filters to find the right mentor for a founder:

1. Click **Filter** at the top of the People view
2. Common filters:
   - **Expertise** = Sales → find mentors who can help with sales
   - **Industry** = Fintech → find mentors in fintech
   - **Availability** = Active mentor → see who's already engaged
   - **Founder Stage** = Seed → find mentors experienced with seed-stage companies
   - **Facilitator** = your name → see your own connections

You can combine multiple filters. For example: Expertise contains "Fundraising" AND Industry contains "SaaS/Tech" AND Availability = "Not contacted".

### Saved Views
Save filter combinations you use often:
1. Set up your filters
2. Click **Save view** (or the view name dropdown)
3. Name it (e.g., "Fintech Fundraising Mentors" or "My Connections")

Suggested views to create:
- **My Connections** — Facilitator = [your name]
- **Active Mentors** — Availability = Active mentor
- **Needs Outreach** — Availability = Not contacted, sorted by Mentorship Score (high to low)
- **By Expertise** — one view per expertise area you focus on

## Understanding the Fields

| Field | What it means |
|-------|---------------|
| **Contact Type** | Mentor, Ecosystem Partner, Both, or Investor |
| **Expertise** | What they're good at: Sales, Marketing, Technology, Engineering, Fundraising, Investor, Connections, Operations |
| **Industry** | Their industry: Fintech, HealthTech, SaaS/Tech, E-commerce/Retail, etc. |
| **Availability** | Where they are in the outreach pipeline (see below) |
| **Mentorship Score** | 1-10 rating of how valuable this mentor could be |
| **Founder Stage** | Which stage founders they're best for: Idea, Pre-seed, Seed, Series A+ |
| **Facilitator** | Which DAM team member owns this relationship (Krish, Sean, Kellen, or Joao) |
| **Mentor Notes** | Free text notes about this person |
| **Source List** | Which original spreadsheet(s) this contact came from |

### Availability Pipeline

This tracks where each contact is in the outreach process:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Not contacted** | Haven't reached out yet |
| **Outreach attempted** | Sent an email/LinkedIn message, waiting for response |
| **Agreed to mentor** | They said yes, need to schedule/onboard |
| **Active mentor** | Currently mentoring DAM founders |
| **Too busy** | Interested but can't commit right now |
| **Connections only** | Willing to make introductions but not mentor directly |
| **Not strong enough** | Not a good fit for our program |

Update this field as you progress through outreach.

## Managing Contacts

### Viewing a Contact
Click on any person's name to see their full record: name, company, LinkedIn, email, plus all the custom fields above.

### Editing a Contact
Click on any field value to edit it inline. Changes save automatically.

### Key actions on a contact record:
- **Email** — Send or draft an email (requires Gmail connected)
- **Notes** — Add to Mentor Notes with context from your conversations
- **Update Availability** — Change their pipeline status as outreach progresses
- **Set Facilitator** — Claim this contact as your connection

## Email Outreach

### Sending a One-Off Email
1. Open a person's record
2. Click the email compose area
3. Write your message and send

### Using Workflows (Automated Outreach)
Go to **Automations** in the left sidebar to see available workflows.

**Draft Email Workflow** (for 1:1 outreach):
- Opens on a single contact record
- Creates a Gmail draft for you to review before sending
- Good for personalized mentor outreach

**Bulk Send Workflow** (for announcements/newsletters):
- Finds all contacts matching criteria
- Sends the same email to each one with a delay between sends
- Good for event invitations or program updates

## Best Practices

1. **Always set Facilitator** when you're reaching out to someone. This prevents two team members from contacting the same person.

2. **Update Availability immediately** after any outreach. If you sent an email, change from "Not contacted" to "Outreach attempted."

3. **Add Mentor Notes** after every interaction. Future you (and your teammates) will thank you.

4. **Use Mentorship Score** to prioritize. When you have limited time, focus on high-score contacts first.

5. **Check existing outreach first.** Before contacting someone, check their Availability status and Facilitator field. If someone else is already working this contact, coordinate with them.

6. **Filter by Source List** to understand context. A contact from "A-Tier" or "Spinelli Connects" may need a different approach than one from "Alumni Founders."
